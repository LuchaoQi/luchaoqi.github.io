Farewell to the Johns Hopkins University

I gained invaluable experience and knowledge at Hopkins. I’m always proud of being a blue jay and being grateful to our university.

During those two years, I’ve met a lot of great people including Dan, Remy, Eric, Stanley, Mark, Ciprian, etc. Feel free to let me know if you think I might have missed you :)

I wanted to express my special thanks to Dr. Brian Caffo. I learned not just knowledge but a lot of life lessons from him, from which I will definitely benefit for the rest of my life.

I’m sure we all incurred some challenges along this journey, and we should believe We have to be greater than what we suffer.

Hope it’s never too late to say thanks to everyone who helped me during my master’s and good-bye to JHU.

On – can congratulations on all graduates’ degree and enjoy the commencement – I’m confident you will find an opportunity that you will thrive in.
