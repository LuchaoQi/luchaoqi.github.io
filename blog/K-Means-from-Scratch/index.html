K-Means from Scratch

K-means is an unsupervised clustering method with steps:

  1. randomly pick K data points as centroids
  2. for each data point, assign it to the closest centroid
  3. update each centroid with the mean of assigned data points
  4. repeat step 2,3 until new centroids and old centroids are merged
